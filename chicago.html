Chicago

The best architecture in the Midwest

Welcome to Chicago!

Chicago, on Lake Michigan in Illinois, is among the largest cities in the U.S. Famed for its bold architecture, it has a skyline punctuated by skyscrapers such as the iconic John Hancock Center, 1,451-ft. Willis Tower (formerly the Sears Tower) and the neo-Gothic Tribune Tower. The city is also renowned for its museums, including the Art Institute of Chicago with its noted Impressionist and Post-Impressionist works.
- Google

Frozen Bean @ Cloud Gate

The shiny bean is overrated; the frozen bean is where it's at!

Pizza Grinders @ Chicago Pizza and Oven Grinder

The pizza pot pie is so much better than deep-dish, not as greasy and filling and so fun to watch.

Chicago Hot Dog @ Portillo's

Just eat everything here, it's so good. But don't put ketchup on the hot dog. It's a crime here!

Tea Service @ 3 Arts Cafe

This place is magical, for service and for location. So cheap too! Spoil yourself, boys and girls.

Flora @ Lincoln Park Conservatory

There are hundreds of types of flowers and plants in this huge conservatory. Wander around, get lost and breathe in clean air!

Fountain and Fireworks @ Navy Pier

There are fireworks every Wednesday and Saturday evening during the summer so check those nights out. Sit by the fountan too!

Sculptures @ Museum of Contemporary Art

The newest galleries have brand new modern and contemporary art installations. A must see!

Wild Berry Pancakes @ Wildberry Cafe

These fluffy pancakes are heavenly and just the right amount of sweet! Definitely worth the wait.

Octopus @ Purple Pig

One of the best dishes at this restaurant, and it's pig-focused restaurant too!
