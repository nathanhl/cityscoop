Madrid

Siestas and tapas, forever.

Welcome to Madrid!

Madrid, Spain's central capital, is a city of elegant boulevards and expansive, manicured parks such as the Buen Retiro. It’s renowned for its rich repositories of European art, including the Prado Museum’s works by Goya, Velázquez and other Spanish masters. The heart of old Hapsburg Madrid is the portico-lined Plaza Mayor, and nearby is the baroque Royal Palace and Armory, displaying historic weaponry.
- Google

Underwater Dining @ Ithaa Undersea Restaurant

This amazing restaurant lets you eat right under the reef and see all the fish swimming by. A definite must-try!

Sea Swings @ Bangtan Resort and Spa

We booked this resort because of the views, and found out that one of their private beaches has a swingset installed right along the shoreline! Instagram worthy!

Personal Docks @ Sheraton Maldives Resort

We loves staying at this resort because each bungalow has its own dock where you can watch the fish swim by. My girlfriend and I loved being able to sip cocktails and splash around, just steps away from our room!

Bioluminescence @ Dusit Thani

If you're lucky, there are bioluminescent blooms that appear on the beaches from late summer to early winter. These are phytoplankton that make the ocean glow in the dark. It's amazing the the water twinkle like the stars in the sky.

Kayaking @ Dusit Thani

Definitely go kayaking on the open ocean. They're cheap to rent and lots of fun to just dip out and go snorkeling. Just be sure to pack lots of sunscreen so you don't get burned.

Infinity Pool @ Turango Resort

This is the most beautiful pool I have ever seen. You have to visit at sunset, when the reflection of the setting sun reflects on the edge of this pool. Feels like you're melting into the sun.

Shark Diving @ Selene's Shark Diving

Although it might seem a little scary, it's definitely worth the experience. The sharks swarm all around you, and with the right protection, you'll get to see these awesome creatures swirling around you.

Surfing @ Maldives Surfing Club

The instructors here are awesome, you'll be swirling around the waters in no time!
